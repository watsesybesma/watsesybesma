Biography

Welcome to my personal website! I work as a postdoctoral research fellow in the field of theoretical physics at the university of Iceland in Reykjavik. I am happy to be supported through my own grant, received from the Icelandic Research Fund.

Currently my work focusses on studying hydrodynamics in absence of boost-symmetry and the application of non-relativistic holography.

In 2017, I obtained my PhD degree under the supervision of Stefan Vandoren, at Utrecht University in the Netherlands. In between obtaining my PhD degree and starting my job in Iceland, I worked for four months at the Utrecht branch of PA consulting group. At PA, I advised various companies based on machine learning analysis and Bayesian statistics. I hope to be able to combine some of my corporate experiences with academic research.

During my time as a PhD candidate I participated in scientific outreach efforts. Not only did I learn a lot from this, but my view also is that it’s important for the field of science to keep on communicating what it is we’re doing to whoever is interested, especially in the age of alternative facts.
